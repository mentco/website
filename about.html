About

Competencies

Expertise and Prestige

Texas-based with decades of manufacturing experience, we sign binding NDAs and confidentiality agreements from San Antonio, operating in sync with the time zone of many of our customers.

Operational Efficiency

Mentco Inc. delivers 25%-50% cost savings compared to domestic suppliers, providing end-to-end management from sourcing to export logistics, maintaining strong supplier relationships, ensuring on-time deliveries, and offering Kanban programs out of San Antonio, Texas or Mumbai, India.

Quality and Reliability

We ensure traceability and serve Fortune 500 corporations, supported by references. With on-site experts and best practices, we offer comprehensive capabilities in engineering, project management, programming, operations, quality assurance, logistics, and various third-party tests and outside processes to meet customer requirements.

Mission

To manufacture the highest quality parts and components from stainless steel, high-performance alloys, and aluminum and brass, and deliver them on time at a reduced price.
