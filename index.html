About Mentco, Inc.

Our headquarters are located in San Antonio, Texas and serves as the hub for our U.S. customers. Our domestic team manages customer relationships, production planning, logistics, and billing. Our Texas facilities include a 10,000 square foot warehouse for just-in-time product delivery, replenished from our offshore manufacturing base. We operate a US-India hybrid model with Mentco's manufacturing center located in Mumbai, India, which is comprised of 35,000 square foot of state-of-the-art CNC turning and milling facilities managed by a highly qualified technical team.

A Message From the President

I am proud to say that, throughout our entire company history, we have never lost a single client.
