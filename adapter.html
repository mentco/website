Adapter CNC Turning

Part Description: Adapter. A CNC lathe was used to manufacture the parts. The overall part dimensions were 1.375" A/F Hex. The tightest tolerance accomplished was ± 0.002". 316 Stainless Steel was used as the material. A 32Ra material finish was achieved. The part was to be used for the Oil and Gas industry.
